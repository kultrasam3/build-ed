What is the BED Test?

The Build an Ethical Driver (BED) Test is a pioneering virtual assessment focused on training individuals to effectively deal with road rage and develop responsible driving behaviors.

Realistic Scenarios

Experience common road rage situations in a safe, virtual environment.

Skill Development

Learn and practice effective techniques for de-escalating and avoiding road rage.

Pre-License Preparation

Gain crucial emotional intelligence and conflict resolution skills before hitting the road.

Why Does It Matter?

Road rage is a significant factor in accidents and unsafe driving environments. The BED Test is crucial for:

  • Increasing Safety: Reducing aggressive behaviors that lead to accidents.
  • Promoting Calm: Equipping drivers with strategies to remain calm under pressure.
  • Building Confidence: Preparing new drivers for challenging on-road interactions.
  • Creating Responsible Drivers: Fostering a culture of ethical and considerate driving.
